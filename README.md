# The Good Neighbor Protocol for Build and Dev Handoffs

> **Good neighbors bring casseroles, not chaos.**

This repo currently focuses on the Build and Dev Handoffs edition of the Good Neighbor Protocol.

This edition focuses on helping founders, clients, developers, and teams turn messy build requests into clearer, kinder, more carryable collaboration. The full Build and Dev Handoffs handbook is available here: https://tennyogodde.gumroad.com/l/eowoat

It is a shared language. A collaboration protocol. A bridge between vision and execution.

---

## The Problem

Most handoffs fail before they begin.

Vague tickets. Rushed asks. Moving goalposts. Hidden assumptions. Requests that force the person on the receiving end to guess what "done" actually means — and then redo it when they guess wrong.

GNP exists to reduce that kind of preventable chaos. Not by making people robotic. Not by burying every idea in paperwork. By giving the request enough shape to actually travel.

A casserole is not perfection. A casserole is something prepared enough to be carried. It has shape, care, thought, and structure. Chaos is when someone dumps ingredients on another person and expects them to cook the whole meal while also guessing what dinner was supposed to be.

**Clarity is kindness.**

---

## What GNP Is

- A **framework** for structuring handoffs and requests
- A **collaboration protocol** for teams, partners, and cross-functional work
- A **shared language** that reduces friction before a project even starts
- A **bridge** between the person who has the idea and the person who has to carry it
- A **kindness-through-structure** model — because the receiving side deserves protection too

## What GNP Is Not

- Not a replacement for Jira, GitHub, Slack, Asana, Linear, ClickUp, Trello, or email
- Not a full software platform — yet
- Not an attempt to make people robotic or every idea arrive perfect
- Not open source at this time

---

## Who It's For

GNP is for founders, clients, developers, project managers, designers, operators, agencies, nonprofits, community programs, and small businesses — anyone where one person needs to hand something meaningful to another person.

It is especially useful where people are trying to ask for something to be built, explain a feature, request a workflow change, clarify scope, reduce confusion, stop preventable rework, or avoid burning out the people receiving the work.

---

## The CASSEROLE Framework

CASSEROLE is the mnemonic at the heart of GNP. Each letter names something a request should be able to answer before it leaves your hands.

| Letter | Element | What it asks |
|--------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **C** | Clarity | Is the ask understandable without guessing? |
| **A** | Alignment | Are we solving the same problem? |
| **S** | Scope | What is actually included in this ask? |
| **S** | Safety | What sensitive stakes are involved? |
| **E** | Expectations | What does "done" actually mean here? |
| **R** | Reasonability | Is this realistic given time, resources, and complexity? |
| **O** | Ownership | Who is asking, carrying, deciding, and accountable? |
| **L** | Limits | What boundaries need to be named before this expands? |
| **E** | Explain-Why | What reasoning supports this request? |

See [docs/casserole-checklist.md](docs/casserole-checklist.md) for a full walkthrough.

## Current Status

The Good Neighbor Protocol for Build and Dev Handoffs currently exists as a **handbook, framework, and live companion GPT**. This repository is its public home.

There is no app. No paid tier. No integrations. The foundation is the Build and Dev Handoffs handbook, and the handbook is here.

## GNP Intake Assistant

The GNP Intake Assistant is now live as a companion GPT.

It helps users turn vague requests into clearer handoffs, check casserole-readiness, identify missing ingredients, and suggest another casserole pass when needed.

**Try it here:** [GNP Intake Assistant](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69e55335a8e4819186385d67a74ab6f0-gnp-intake-assistant)

It is an early interactive layer of GNP — a real, working tool — but not yet the full structured app or middleware vision. See [docs/gpt-intake-assistant.md](docs/gpt-intake-assistant.md) for more detail.
